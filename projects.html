CLAS12 Run Group D SIDIS Analysis

A nuclear SIDIS analysis using deuterium, carbon, copper, and tin targets. The workflow includes event selection, electron identification, positive-pion selection, kinematic calculations, detector studies, and extraction of nuclear observables.
